About Comstex GmbH

Founded in 2003, Comstex GmbH has grown into a leading provider of telecommunications infrastructure and solutions. Headquartered in Germany, we serve clients worldwide with state-of-the-art products and services tailored to enterprise and service provider needs.

We value integrity, innovation, and customer-centricity. Our team of engineers and industry experts work tirelessly to develop solutions that align with the evolving needs of modern enterprises.

Our Journey

  • 2003: Company founded in Berlin.
  • 2010: Launched our flagship network security appliance.
  • 2015: Partnered with Cisco as a certified distributor.
  • 2020: Expanded operations to Asia-Pacific region.
  • 2023: Recognized as industry leader in secure telecom solutions.

Comstex continues to innovate with R&D centers exploring next-generation networking technologies, AI-driven security, and cloud-native architectures.
