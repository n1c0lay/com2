About Comstex

Founded in 2003, Comstex has grown into a leading provider of telecommunications infrastructure and solutions. Headquartered in Germany, we serve clients worldwide with state-of-the-art products and services tailored to enterprise and service provider needs.

Our mission is to simplify and enhance global connectivity by delivering innovative networking hardware, secure communication platforms, and comprehensive support to ensure seamless operations.

Our Journey

  • 2003: Company founded in Berlin.
  • 2006: First international office opened in London.
  • 2010: Launched our flagship network security appliance.
  • 2015: Partnered with Cisco as a certified distributor.
  • 2020: Expanded operations to Asia-Pacific region.
  • 2023: Recognized as industry leader in secure telecom solutions.

Comstex continues to innovate with R&D centers exploring next-generation networking technologies, AI-driven security, and cloud-native architectures.
